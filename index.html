Hello! I'm Caryl, a Sales Administration and Operations professional with a strong interest in data analytics and business intelligence.

Throughout my career, I have worked with sales reporting, operational processes, customer data, and business documentation, where accuracy and data-driven decision-making play an important role. This experience sparked my passion for analyzing data and transforming information into actionable insights. I am currently developing my expertise in Microsoft Excel, Power Query, data cleaning, dashboard development, KPI reporting, and data visualization. I enjoy identifying trends, improving reporting processes, and creating dashboards that help businesses monitor performance and make informed decisions.

I believe data should do more than exist — it should communicate. My approach blends analytical thinking with a designer's eye to transform raw data into intuitive dashboards, insightful reports, and engaging visualizations. I am passionate about turning business information into stories that drive better decisions and create measurable impact.
